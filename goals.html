Goal: Discover Myself

Personal Portfolios

The following three portfolios represent my personal and professional self-assessment, developed through reflection on my academic journey, co-op experience, and extracurricular involvement. Together, they form a holistic picture of who I am and where I am headed.

Portfolio I

Dreams and Goals

My primary career goal is to architect highly scalable software systems that solve complex quantitative problems. The discipline required to reach this goal is something I have cultivated both inside and outside the classroom. Recently, completing an 18-week training block to run the Toronto Waterfront Marathon taught me that long-term success is built on incremental, daily execution. I apply this same endurance to my academic timeline and professional development, understanding that mastering software engineering is a marathon, not a sprint.

Portfolio II

Interests and Skills

I possess a strong interest in generative AI and workflow automation, paired with highly developed organizational skills. As Head Event Coordinator for the ski club, I successfully managed the logistics, budgeting, and safety protocols for a massive trip to Mont Tremblant for over 100 students. This experience drastically changed my perspective on engineering: I realized that managing complex, moving parts in real-world events perfectly mirrors the logic required to design clean backend system architecture. Both require anticipating edge cases, managing resources, and ensuring user safety.

Portfolio III

Values and Personality

I value analytical rigor paired with creative problem-solving. My current role as a Treasury Analyst at PC Financial requires strict adherence to financial discipline and quantitative accuracy. However, my freelance work as a full-stack developer allows me to exercise creativity in building user interfaces and optimizing user experiences. I believe my strongest personality trait is adaptability—being able to seamlessly switch between the strict logic of mathematical modeling and the creative design of web development.
